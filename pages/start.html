How To Get Started

There are many ways one can get started with the Lightning Network. With the Strike app one can send payments to other users anywhere in the world, similar to Venmo or Cash app. Strike is also integrated with Twitter now so you can transact with anyone who has linked their Strike and Twitter accounts together. One can also use a Podcasting 2.0 app such as Breez to listen to their favorite podcasts and send sats to support their favorite creator, or just use it as an everday Lightning Wallet. These are custodial wallet options, but people can use non custodial wallets such as Zeus or Zap for more privacy and security.
